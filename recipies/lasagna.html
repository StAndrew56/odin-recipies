Lasagna Recipe

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.
You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain.
  3. In a pan, cook ground beef until browned; drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the beef and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce.
  7. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and repeat.
  8. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Bake for 30-40 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  10. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
