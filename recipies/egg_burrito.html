Egg Burrito

Do you enjoy egg burritos? Great! Did you know it's actually very simple to make
your own burrito in the comfort of your house. This recipe will show
you exactly how to create your own!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 large flour tortilla
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or your choice)
  • Salt & black pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tsp butter or oil (for cooking eggs)
  • Optional: salsa, avocado, cooked bacon, or sausage

Step by Step

  1. Heat a nonstick pan over medium heat.
  2. Scramble 2 eggs with salt and pepper.
  3. Warm a tortilla on the pan for 30 seconds.
  4. Spread cheese on the tortilla and add scrambled eggs.
  5. (Optional) Add salsa, avocado, or cooked bacon.
  6. Roll the tortilla into a burrito and toast it in the pan for 30 seconds per side.
  7. Enjoy!
