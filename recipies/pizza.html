Pizza

Do you enjoy Pizza? Great! Did you know it's actually very simple to make
your own Pizza in the comfort of your house. This recipe will show
you exactly how to create your own!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Pepperoni, mushrooms, or any toppings you like
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (for brushing the crust)

Step by Step

  1. 1 lb pizza Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and add toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.).
  5. Transfer to the hot baking sheet and bake for 10-15 minutes until crust is golden.
  6. Remove from oven, let cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy!
